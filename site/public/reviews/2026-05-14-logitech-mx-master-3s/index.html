Logitech MX Master 3S Review

Last updated: May 14, 2026

If you’re in the market for a high-performance mouse that offers comfort, precision, and versatility, the Logitech MX Master 3S is definitely worth considering. In this review, we’ll delve into the key features, pros, and cons of this advanced mouse, helping you decide if it’s the right fit for your needs. Whether you’re a gamer, a graphic designer, or simply someone who spends a lot of time working on a computer, this review aims to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of what the Logitech MX Master 3S has to offer.

Key Features

The Logitech MX Master 3S boasts an array of impressive features that set it apart from other mice on the market. One of its most notable features is the silent click mechanism, which ensures that your work or gaming sessions are not interrupted by loud clicking sounds. The ergonomic design provides a comfortable fit in your hand, reducing fatigue during extended use. The mouse also supports multi-device pairing, allowing you to seamlessly switch between up to three devices. The MagSpeed scroll wheel is another standout feature, offering incredibly fast and precise scrolling. With a battery life of up to 70 days on a single charge, you can enjoy uninterrupted performance without worrying about running out of power.

Pros and Cons

Like any product, the Logitech MX Master 3S has its pros and cons. On the positive side, the mouse offers exceptional comfort and precision, making it ideal for long working hours or intense gaming sessions. The silent click feature is a significant advantage for those who work in quiet environments or prefer a more discreet computing experience. The multi-device support and long battery life are also major pluses. However, some users may find the mouse to be slightly heavy, and the price point may be higher than what some buyers are willing to pay. Additionally, the software required for customizing the mouse’s settings can be somewhat complex for novice users.

Who Is It Best For?

The Logitech MX Master 3S is best suited for individuals who require a high level of precision and comfort in their computing experience. This includes gamers who need quick and accurate cursor movement, graphic designers who require precise control over their work, and professionals who spend extended periods working on their computers. The mouse’s ergonomic design and silent click mechanism also make it an excellent choice for those who work in quiet environments or prefer a more discreet computing experience.

Price and Value

The Logitech MX Master 3S is priced at a premium level, reflecting its advanced features and high-quality construction. While it may be more expensive than some other mice on the market, the value it offers in terms of performance, comfort, and durability makes it a worthwhile investment for those who prioritize these aspects. If you’re looking for a long-term solution that can enhance your computing experience, the Logitech MX Master 3S is certainly worth considering.

Final Verdict

In conclusion, the Logitech MX Master 3S is an exceptional mouse that offers a unique blend of comfort, precision, and versatility. While it may have some drawbacks, such as its weight and price, the benefits it provides make it an excellent choice for those who value high-performance computing. If you’re in the market for a premium mouse that can enhance your gaming, work, or overall computing experience, we highly recommend checking out the Logitech MX Master 3S.
