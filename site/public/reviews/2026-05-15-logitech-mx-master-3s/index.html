Logitech MX Master 3S Review

Last updated: May 15, 2026

The Logitech MX Master 3S is a high-end computer mouse designed to provide comfort, precision, and versatility. In this review, we’ll delve into the key features, pros, and cons of this premium mouse, helping you decide if it’s the right choice for your needs. Whether you’re a professional, gamer, or simply looking for a reliable mouse, this article will cover everything you need to know about the Logitech MX Master 3S.

Key Features

The Logitech MX Master 3S boasts an impressive array of features that set it apart from other computer mice. Some of the most notable features include silent clicks, which provide a quiet and discreet typing experience, an ergonomic design that fits comfortably in the hand, and multi-device support, allowing you to connect up to three devices at once. The mouse also features a MagSpeed scroll wheel, which provides fast and precise scrolling, and a long-lasting battery that can last up to 70 days on a single charge.

Pros and Cons

Like any product, the Logitech MX Master 3S has its pros and cons. On the plus side, the mouse is incredibly comfortable to use, with a contoured design that fits perfectly in the hand. The silent clicks are also a major advantage, providing a quiet and distraction-free typing experience. The mouse is also highly customizable, with a range of buttons and gestures that can be programmed to suit your needs. However, some users may find the mouse to be slightly heavy, and the price tag is certainly on the higher side. Additionally, some users have reported issues with the mouse’s connectivity, although this is relatively rare.

Who Is It Best For?

The Logitech MX Master 3S is a versatile mouse that can be used by a wide range of people. Professionals, such as graphic designers, video editors, and programmers, will appreciate the mouse’s precision and customization options. Gamers will also love the mouse’s fast and accurate tracking, as well as its customizable buttons and gestures. Additionally, anyone who spends long hours working on a computer will appreciate the mouse’s comfort and ergonomic design.

Price and Value

The Logitech MX Master 3S is a premium product with a price tag to match. At around $100, it’s certainly not the cheapest mouse on the market. However, when you consider the mouse’s advanced features, comfortable design, and long-lasting battery, it’s clear that it offers excellent value for money. Whether you’re a professional, gamer, or simply looking for a high-quality mouse, the Logitech MX Master 3S is definitely worth considering.

Final Verdict

In conclusion, the Logitech MX Master 3S is an excellent computer mouse that offers a range of advanced features, comfortable design, and long-lasting battery. While it may be slightly pricey, it’s clear that it offers excellent value for money. If you’re in the market for a high-quality mouse that can keep up with your demanding needs, the Logitech MX Master 3S is definitely worth considering.
