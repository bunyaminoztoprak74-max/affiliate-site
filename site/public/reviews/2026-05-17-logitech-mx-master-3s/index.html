Logitech MX Master 3S Review

Last updated: May 17, 2026

The search for the perfect mouse can be a daunting task, especially with the numerous options available in the market. However, if you’re looking for a mouse that combines comfort, precision, and versatility, the Logitech MX Master 3S is definitely worth considering. In this review, we’ll delve into the key features, pros, and cons of this wireless mouse, helping you make an informed decision on whether it’s the right fit for your needs.

Key Features

The Logitech MX Master 3S boasts an array of impressive features that set it apart from its competitors. One of its standout features is the silent click technology, which provides a quiet and discreet typing experience. The ergonomic design ensures a comfortable fit in the hand, reducing fatigue and strain during extended use. The mouse also features multi-device support, allowing you to seamlessly switch between up to three devices with the touch of a button. The MagSpeed scroll wheel is another notable feature, offering fast and precise scrolling with a satisfying click. Additionally, the mouse has a long-lasting battery life of up to 70 days on a single charge.

Pros and Cons

Like any product, the Logitech MX Master 3S has its pros and cons. On the positive side, the mouse offers exceptional comfort and precision, making it ideal for heavy users. The silent click technology is also a significant advantage, especially in quiet environments. The multi-device support and MagSpeed scroll wheel are also notable benefits. However, some users may find the mouse to be slightly larger than expected, which can take some time to get used to. Additionally, the price point may be a deterrent for some, as it’s positioned as a premium product.

Who Is It Best For?

The Logitech MX Master 3S is best suited for individuals who spend a significant amount of time on their computers, such as programmers, graphic designers, and writers. The ergonomic design and silent click technology make it an excellent choice for those who value comfort and discretion. The multi-device support also makes it an ideal option for individuals who work with multiple devices simultaneously. Additionally, the mouse is suitable for users who prioritize precision and accuracy, such as gamers and video editors.

Price and Value

The Logitech MX Master 3S is priced at a premium level, which may be a barrier for some potential buyers. However, considering the exceptional features and performance, the mouse offers excellent value for the price. The long-lasting battery life, durable construction, and advanced features make it a worthwhile investment for heavy users. If you’re looking for a high-quality mouse that can keep up with your demanding workflow, the Logitech MX Master 3S is definitely worth considering.
