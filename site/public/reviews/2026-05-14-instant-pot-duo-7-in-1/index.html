Instant Pot Duo Review

Last updated: May 14, 2026

If you’re in the market for a versatile and efficient kitchen appliance, you’ve likely come across the Instant Pot Duo 7-in-1. This review will cover the key features, pros, and cons of the Instant Pot Duo, helping you decide if it’s the right addition to your kitchen. The Instant Pot Duo has been a game-changer for many home cooks, and for good reason. With its ability to replace seven common kitchen appliances, it’s a space-saving and convenient solution for cooking a variety of dishes. In this review, we’ll dive into the details of the Instant Pot Duo, exploring its features, benefits, and drawbacks, to help you make an informed decision.

Key Features

The Instant Pot Duo 7-in-1 is a multifunctional appliance that combines the functions of a pressure cooker, slow cooker, rice cooker, steamer, saute pan, yogurt maker, and food warmer. Its 6-quart capacity makes it ideal for cooking for 4-6 people, and the 13 one-touch programs provide ease of use for a variety of dishes. The Instant Pot Duo also features 10 safety mechanisms, including automatic shut-off and a secure lid, to ensure safe and worry-free cooking. Additionally, the inner pot is dishwasher-safe, making cleaning a breeze.

Pros and Cons

One of the standout benefits of the Instant Pot Duo is its ability to cook food up to 70% faster than traditional methods. This is especially useful for busy households where time is of the essence. The Instant Pot Duo is also incredibly versatile, allowing you to cook a wide range of dishes, from soups and stews to rice and yogurt. However, some users have reported that the Instant Pot Duo can be noisy during operation, and the non-stick coating on the inner pot may wear off over time. Additionally, the Instant Pot Duo is a relatively large appliance, so it may not be suitable for smaller kitchens.

Who Is It Best For?

The Instant Pot Duo 7-in-1 is ideal for home cooks who value convenience, versatility, and efficiency in their kitchen appliances. It’s perfect for busy households, families with multiple children, and individuals who enjoy cooking a variety of dishes. The Instant Pot Duo is also a great option for those who are new to pressure cooking or slow cooking, as it provides an easy-to-use interface and a wide range of preset programs.

Price and Value

The Instant Pot Duo 7-in-1 is generally priced around $100, which is a relatively affordable price point considering its multifunctional capabilities. When compared to other pressure cookers or slow cookers on the market, the Instant Pot Duo offers excellent value for its price. Additionally, the Instant Pot Duo is a durable appliance that can withstand regular use, making it a worthwhile investment for any home cook.

Final Verdict

In conclusion, the Instant Pot Duo 7-in-1 is an excellent addition to any kitchen. Its versatility, efficiency, and ease of use make it a standout appliance, and its affordable price point makes it an accessible option for home cooks of all levels. While it may have some minor drawbacks, the benefits of the Instant Pot Duo far outweigh its limitations.
