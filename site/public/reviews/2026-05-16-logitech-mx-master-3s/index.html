Key Features

The Logitech MX Master 3S is packed with innovative features that set it apart from other mice on the market. Some of its key features include:

  • Ergonomic design: The MX Master 3S is designed to fit comfortably in your hand, with a contoured shape and soft-touch surface that reduces fatigue and discomfort.
  • Multi-device support: The mouse can connect to up to three devices at once, allowing you to easily switch between your computer, laptop, and tablet.
