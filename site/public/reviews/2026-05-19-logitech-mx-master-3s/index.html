Logitech MX Master 3S Review

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Are you in the market for a high-performance mouse that combines comfort, precision, and versatility? Look no further than the Logitech MX Master 3S. In this review, we’ll take a closer look at the features, pros, and cons of this popular mouse, helping you decide if it’s the right fit for your needs. From its silent clicks to its MagSpeed scroll wheel, we’ll cover everything you need to know about the Logitech MX Master 3S.

Key Features

The Logitech MX Master 3S boasts an impressive array of features that make it an attractive option for professionals, gamers, and anyone looking for a high-quality mouse. Some of its key features include silent clicks, which provide a quiet and discreet typing experience, an ergonomic design that fits comfortably in the hand, and multi-device support, allowing you to connect up to three devices at once. The mouse also features a MagSpeed scroll wheel, which provides fast and precise scrolling, and a long-lasting battery that can last up to 70 days on a single charge.

Pros and Cons

Like any product, the Logitech MX Master 3S has its pros and cons. On the plus side, the mouse is incredibly comfortable to use, with a contoured design that fits perfectly in the hand. The silent clicks are also a major advantage, providing a quiet and distraction-free typing experience. Additionally, the MagSpeed scroll wheel is fast and precise, making it perfect for scrolling through long documents or web pages. On the downside, the mouse is a bit pricey, with a higher cost than some other options on the market. Additionally, some users may find the mouse to be a bit heavy, which can take some getting used to.

Who Is It Best For?

The Logitech MX Master 3S is a versatile mouse that can be used by a wide range of individuals. Professionals, such as graphic designers, video editors, and programmers, will appreciate the mouse’s precision and comfort, making it perfect for long hours of work. Gamers will also love the mouse’s fast and precise tracking, making it ideal for fast-paced games. Additionally, anyone looking for a high-quality mouse for everyday use will find the Logitech MX Master 3S to be a great option.

Price and Value

The Logitech MX Master 3S is priced at around $100, which is a bit higher than some other options on the market. However, considering the mouse’s high-quality features, comfort, and durability, it’s well worth the investment. The mouse’s long-lasting battery and durable design also make it a great value in the long run, as it can withstand heavy use and last for years to come.

Final Verdict

In conclusion, the Logitech MX Master 3S is an excellent choice for anyone looking for a high-performance mouse that combines comfort, precision, and versatility. With its silent clicks, ergonomic design, and MagSpeed scroll wheel, it’s perfect for professionals, gamers, and anyone looking for a high-quality mouse. While it may be a bit pricey, the mouse’s durability and long-lasting battery make it a great value in the long run.
