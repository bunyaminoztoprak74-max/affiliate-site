Logitech MX Master 3S Review

Last updated: May 18, 2026

The Logitech MX Master 3S is a highly-regarded mouse designed to cater to the needs of both gamers and productivity enthusiasts. This review will delve into the key features, pros, and cons of the Logitech MX Master 3S, helping you determine if it’s the right fit for your computing needs.

Key Features

The Logitech MX Master 3S boasts an impressive array of features that set it apart from other mice on the market. Some of its key features include silent clicks, which provide a quiet and distraction-free typing experience, an ergonomic design that fits comfortably in the hand, and multi-device support, allowing users to seamlessly switch between up to three devices. Additionally, the MagSpeed scroll wheel provides fast and precise scrolling, while the 70-day battery life ensures that you can use the mouse for extended periods without needing to recharge.

Pros and Cons

The Logitech MX Master 3S has several advantages that make it an attractive option for those in the market for a new mouse. Some of its pros include its ergonomic design, which provides a comfortable fit for users with large or small hands, its silent clicks, which are ideal for shared workspaces or home offices, and its MagSpeed scroll wheel, which provides fast and precise scrolling. However, the mouse also has some drawbacks, including its high price point, which may be out of budget for some users, and its lack of customization options, which may be a disappointment for gamers who want to personalize their mouse experience.

Who Is It Best For?

The Logitech MX Master 3S is best suited for individuals who require a high-performance mouse for productivity or gaming. This includes professionals who work in shared workspaces or home offices, gamers who want a mouse that provides fast and precise scrolling, and individuals who value ergonomic design and comfort. Additionally, the mouse is also suitable for users who want to switch between multiple devices, such as a computer, tablet, or smartphone.

Price and Value

The Logitech MX Master 3S is priced at a premium, with a price point that reflects its high-quality features and design. While it may be out of budget for some users, the mouse provides excellent value for those who are willing to invest in a high-performance mouse. With its 70-day battery life, durable construction, and advanced features, the Logitech MX Master 3S is a worthwhile investment for anyone who wants a reliable and efficient mouse.

Final Verdict

In conclusion, the Logitech MX Master 3S is a top-notch mouse that provides a unique combination of comfort, precision, and speed. With its ergonomic design, silent clicks, and MagSpeed scroll wheel, it’s an ideal choice for productivity enthusiasts and gamers alike. If you’re in the market for a high-performance mouse and are willing to invest in a premium product, the Logitech MX Master 3S is definitely worth considering.
