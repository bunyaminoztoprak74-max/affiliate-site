Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Introduction

Affiliate Review Hub (“we”, “our”, “this site”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect when you visit affiliate-site-jade-nine.vercel.app, how we use it, and your rights regarding it.

What We Collect

Analytics Data (Google Analytics 4)

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous usage statistics, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referral source (search engine, social media, direct)
  • General geographic region (country / city — never precise location)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system

This data is aggregated and anonymised. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected by Google Analytics. You can opt out at any time using the GA Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Session Recordings (Microsoft Clarity)

We use Microsoft Clarity to generate heatmaps and anonymised session recordings. Clarity automatically masks form inputs and sensitive content. No PII is captured. Review Microsoft’s Privacy Statement for full details on how Clarity handles data.

Affiliate Click Data

When you click an affiliate link, we log the event anonymously in Google Analytics (product name, page title). We do not receive your name, payment details, or order information from Amazon or any other affiliate network.

  • Names, email addresses, or any contact information
  • Payment or financial data

Third-Party Services

This site links to Amazon.com and other retailers via affiliate links. When you follow an affiliate link, you leave this site and are subject to the privacy policy of the destination site. We recommend reviewing Amazon’s Privacy Notice before making a purchase.

Your Rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Object — opt out of analytics tracking (see GA opt-out link above)
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format

As we collect only anonymous analytics data, we are unlikely to hold any identifiable data about you. For any privacy requests, contact us using the details below.

Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, open an issue or send a message via the site’s GitHub repository.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
