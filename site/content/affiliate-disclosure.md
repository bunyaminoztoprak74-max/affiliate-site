## Affiliate Disclosure

> **Short version:** This site contains affiliate links. If you buy something through

> This never influences our editorial opinions.

## Amazon Associates

Affiliate Review Hub is a participant in the **Amazon Services LLC Associates Program**,
an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn
advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

Our Amazon Associate tag is **`affiliater07c-20`**. Links containing
`tag=affiliater07c-20` in the URL are affiliate links.

## How It Works

1. You click a link on this site labelled "Check Best Price on Amazon" or similar.
2. You are taken to Amazon (or another retailer's) product page.
3. If you complete a qualifying purchase within the cookie window, we earn a
   small percentage commission at **no additional cost to you**.
4. Amazon and other networks track the referral via the associate tag in the URL.

## What We Earn Commissions On

We earn commissions on products we directly review **and** on other products purchased
during the same Amazon session after clicking one of our links (standard Associates
programme behaviour).

## Editorial Independence

Commissions **do not** influence our reviews or recommendations. We research products
independently, use publicly available specifications and user reviews, and aim to give
an honest, balanced assessment of each product's strengths and weaknesses.

Products are selected because they are popular, well-reviewed, or genuinely useful —
not because they carry higher commission rates.
