## Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 14, 2026

### Analytics & Cookies

This site uses **Google Analytics 4** to collect anonymous usage data (pages visited,
session duration, traffic sources). No personally identifiable information is collected.
You can opt out using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

This site may also use **Microsoft Clarity** to record anonymized session replays and
generate heatmaps. Clarity does not collect personal data. See
[Microsoft's Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement) for details.

### Affiliate Links

This site contains affiliate links. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase,
we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. See our
[Affiliate Disclosure](/affiliate-disclosure/) for full details.

### Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect names, email addresses, payment information, or any other personally
identifiable data. We do not sell data to third parties.
