## Introduction

Affiliate Review Hub ("we", "our", "this site") is committed to protecting your privacy.
This policy explains what data we collect when you visit
**affiliate-site-jade-nine.vercel.app**, how we use it, and your rights regarding it.

## What We Collect

### Analytics Data (Google Analytics 4)

We use **Google Analytics 4** to collect anonymous usage statistics, including:

- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Referral source (search engine, social media, direct)
- General geographic region (country / city — never precise location)
- Device type, browser, and operating system

This data is aggregated and anonymised. No personally identifiable information (PII)
is collected by Google Analytics. You can opt out at any time using the
[GA Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### Session Recordings (Microsoft Clarity)

We use **Microsoft Clarity** to generate heatmaps and anonymised session recordings.
Clarity automatically masks form inputs and sensitive content. No PII is captured.
Review [Microsoft's Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement)
for full details on how Clarity handles data.

### Affiliate Click Data

When you click an affiliate link, we log the event anonymously in Google Analytics
(product name, page title). We do **not** receive your name, payment details, or order
information from Amazon or any other affiliate network.

## Cookies

| Cookie type | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| `_ga`, `_ga_*` | Google Analytics visitor identifier | 2 years |
| `_clsk`, `_clck` | Microsoft Clarity session | 1 day / 1 year |

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or sell cookie data to third parties.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- Names, email addresses, or any contact information
- Payment or financial data
- Passwords or account credentials
- Sensitive personal data of any kind

## Third-Party Services

This site links to **Amazon.com** and other retailers via affiliate links. When you
follow an affiliate link, you leave this site and are subject to the privacy policy of
the destination site. We recommend reviewing Amazon's
[Privacy Notice](https://www.amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=GX7NJQ4ZB8MHFRNJ)
before making a purchase.

## Your Rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
- **Erasure** — request deletion of your personal data
- **Object** — opt out of analytics tracking (see GA opt-out link above)
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format

As we collect only anonymous analytics data, we are unlikely to hold any identifiable
data about you. For any privacy requests, contact us using the details below.
